About Sunshine Tempered Glass Ltd.

Sunshine Tempered Glass Ltd. was established in October 2024 and is proudly based in Calgary. Our 21,500 sq. ft. facility is purpose-built to deliver efficient, high-quality glass processing services tailored to modern construction and design needs.

We specialize in tempered glass manufacturing, offering both flat and curved tempered glass solutions. Notably, we are currently the only company in Calgary capable of producing curved tempered glass, allowing us to support complex designs and custom project requirements that others cannot accommodate.

Our facility is equipped with advanced machinery, including precision cutting equipment, CNC fabrication systems, professional washing units, and water filtration systems. These technologies ensure consistent quality, precision, and cleanliness throughout every stage of production.
